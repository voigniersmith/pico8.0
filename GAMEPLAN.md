# Development Phases

**Phase 0:** Project Setup & Movement
*You can move a sprite around the screen*

**Phase 1:** Single Enemy + Combat
*One enemy chases you, you can fight back*

**Phase 2:** Enemy Spawning & Waves
*Enemies spawn continuously, screen fills up*

**Phase 3:** XP & Leveling
*Kill enemies → collect XP → level up*

**Phase 4:** Level-Up Choices
*Level up → choose 1 of 3 upgrades → get stronger*

**Phase 5:** Day/Night Cycle
*Day = enemies flee, Night = enemies attack*

**Phase 6:** Weapons & Auto-Attack
*Weapons fire automatically, stack multiple weapons*

**Phase 7:** Multiple Enemy Types
*Fast enemies, tanky enemies, ranged enemies*

**Phase 8:** Meta-Progression
*Unlock permanent upgrades between runs*

**Phase 9:** Polish & Juice
*Screen shake, particles, sound, feel*

---

*Each phase gets a detailed breakdown only when we start it.*
